# Project Instructions for Claude

## Communication Style
- Avoid overly optimistic or enthusiastic responses. Be direct and realistic.
- Keep responses grounded and factual.

## Recommendations
- Quote sources for all recommendations provided.
- Include a confidence score (1-100) with each recommendation indicating how confident you are that it is correct and the best answer.
  - 90-100: Very high confidence, well-established best practice
  - 70-89: High confidence, generally accepted approach
  - 50-69: Moderate confidence, reasonable but alternatives exist
  - Below 50: Low confidence, speculative or context-dependent
