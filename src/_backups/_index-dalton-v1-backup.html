We're Diego and Trista.

Diego has called St. Augustine home since 2003. Flagler College grad. Years as an RN at Flagler Hospital, recovering patients after open-heart surgery — work that taught him how to stay calm when rooms aren't, put nervous people at ease, and keep the mission front and center. Those instincts shape every shoot day.

Trista holds a PhD and brings strategic planning, communications, and photography to the studio. She's the reason our productions are organized, intentional, and built around what your organization is actually trying to accomplish.

We started First Sight Films in 2023 because we wanted to do creative and strategic work for organizations doing good work. Telling those stories is what drives us.

How it works.

1

We get to know each other.

A short conversation. We learn your organization, your audience, and the moments that matter. We tell you honestly whether we're the right fit.

2

We map a strategic content cadence to your year — anchor pieces, people-focused stories, ongoing short-form work. You see exactly what gets made, when, and how it ships through your compliance and brand review.

3

Cameras and crew show up. People feel comfortable. Footage gets shot, edited, approved, and delivered on a calendar your team can rely on. No fire drills.

Three ways to work together.

We organize our work by the depth of the relationship, not the format of the deliverable. Photo and video, leader portraits, brand pieces, event coverage, recruiting content — all of it lives inside one of the three tiers below.

  • Fully edited photo and/or video deliverables

The Strategic Intensive

A paid strategy engagement and a flagship piece, for organizations considering a longer partnership.

This isn't a consulting deck and it isn't a video project. It's our process applied to figuring out what content your organization actually needs — and producing the anchor piece your team has been meaning to make.

We sit down with your leadership, talk with the people inside your organization, and audit the content you already have. From that, we write a strategic brief: the stories worth telling, who they're for, and the cadence that fits your year. Then we produce one flagship deliverable against that brief. The brief is yours to keep, whether or not you continue with us.

  • Discovery sessions with leadership and stakeholders
  • Audit of existing content and channels
  • Written strategic brief
  • One flagship photo or video deliverable
  • Six to eight weeks

From $8,500

What changes when you have the right partner.

Your week gets quieter. The video questions stop landing on your desk because someone is already handling them. Your people are visible. Your recruits know what your office feels like before they walk in. Your LinkedIn doesn't go dark between campaigns. The board sees consistent work instead of one expensive piece a year. And the people you serve start associating your organization with the real humans doing real work. Not a logo they scroll past.

What it costs to stay where you are.

Another year of trying to fit content into the margins of an already-full week. Another expensive one-off that nobody finishes watching. Another quarter of watching peer organizations show up consistently while yours goes quiet. The organizations in St. Johns and Duval that figure this out first will earn the recognition that comes with consistent, human storytelling. The ones that don't will keep paying for work that disappears.

Why we make work this way.

The thing your audience is really deciding, every time they scroll past you or stop, is whether to trust you. Trust isn't earned by polish. It's earned by recognition — by seeing real people doing real work, consistently, in a way that matches who you say you are.

We started First Sight Films because we believe the most interesting subject in commercial photo and video is the same one that's always been the most interesting subject in any honest piece of work: a person doing something that matters, in a place that means something, with stakes that are real.

That belief is why we work mostly with cultural institutions, civic organizations, and professional firms whose people are the substance of the work. It's why we built the studio around partnerships instead of one-off shoots. And it's why we'll be honest with you when we think you're spending in the wrong place — because we'd rather have a hard conversation early than ship work that doesn't earn the trust your organization has spent years building.

We're not for every organization. But the ones we're for — we're really for.

Let's get one good thing made together. We'll go from there.
