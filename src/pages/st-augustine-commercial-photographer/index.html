Architectural Photography

Project at a glance

  • Clients: St. Augustine Amphitheatre, Ponte Vedra Concert Hall, Fort Mose Historic State Park, Flagler College, Quatrefoil Associates, Walk-On's
  • Session: typically 4–6 hours on-site
  • Lighting: 0 to 4 lights, matched to natural light + intended feel
  • Technique: exposure bracketing for dark interiors, merged in Adobe Lightroom Classic
  • Use: web, print, social

First Sight Films has shot architectural photography for some of Northeast Florida's most recognized spaces. Some frames focus purely on the architecture; others show people interacting with the space, depending on what the image needs to do.

We start with the question that drives everything: where will this live, and what's it for? An ad, an educational or instructional piece, a design concept, a mailer, an email campaign. Sometimes a client doesn't know every use yet, so we shoot to cover them. From there we schedule the session.

How we light it depends on the space and the feel we're after, balanced against the available natural light. For darker interiors like the Ponte Vedra Concert Hall, we use exposure bracketing — capturing the same composition at several exposure levels, then merging them in Adobe Lightroom Classic into one clean, evenly lit frame.

Our Approach to Photography

First Sight Films plans every commercial shoot around one question: where will these images be used? We build the shoot to serve that — an ad, an e-commerce listing, a catalog, a social campaign — so the photos work in the channels you actually need, not just in a gallery.

What sets us apart from a typical photographer:

  • We run full productions, not just a camera. Art direction, production design, and on-set crew — we ran a 7-model, two-day apparel campaign for the St. Augustine Amphitheatre.
  • We start with the end use. We plan around where the image lives — web, print, or social — before the shoot, so you don't pay for photos you can't use.
  • We deliver in 72 hours. Most photographers take one to two weeks; we turn edited galleries around in three days.
  • We capture photo and video in one session. Alongside the stills, we shoot behind-the-scenes footage and short social clips — so you leave with content for every channel, not just a photo gallery.
  • We give you full usage rights. A perpetual license to use the images anywhere, including paid ads, while we handle ownership and archiving.
  • Institutions trust us. We're the photographer for the St. Augustine Amphitheatre, Ponte Vedra Concert Hall, and Sing Out Loud Festival.

And we keep the shoot easy — you stay stress-free while we get the shot. On product and brand work we "fix it in pre-production": plan thoroughly, so we have room to chase what's working on the day.
