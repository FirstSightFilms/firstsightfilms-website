About St. Johns Cultural Council

St. Johns Cultural Council is the designated Local Arts Agency for St. Johns County, serving as the hub for arts, culture, and heritage in the region. The organization supports local artists, coordinates cultural events, and advocates for arts funding throughout the county.

First Sight Films has collaborated with St. Johns Cultural Council on event coverage, promotional videos, and documentary projects that highlight the vibrant arts community in St. Augustine and surrounding areas.

Our work includes coverage of cultural festivals, artist spotlights, and grant program documentation that helps showcase the impact of arts investment in St. Johns County.
