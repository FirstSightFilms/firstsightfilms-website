How First Sight Films Works with The Amp

What began as a video editing partnership in 2022 has grown into a comprehensive creative retainer with the St. Augustine Amphitheatre. First Sight Films now serves as the venue's go-to production team for video, photography, and creative direction across their full calendar of events.

Our monthly retainer covers three core areas: seasonal merchandise campaigns, concert coverage, and festival documentation. For merchandise, we produce lifestyle photo and video content featuring The Amp's apparel and goods—capturing the Florida outdoor aesthetic that resonates with their audience. Concert nights see us capturing artist performances, crowd energy, and behind-the-scenes moments that fuel their social media and promotional efforts.

The partnership extends to St. Augustine's signature festivals, including Sing Out Loud and other multi-day events hosted at The Amp and surrounding venues. From stage performances to vendor activations, we deliver a steady stream of content that keeps the venue connected with its community year-round.

This ongoing collaboration allows us to deeply understand The Amp's brand voice and deliver consistent, on-brand content without the overhead of project-by-project coordination.
