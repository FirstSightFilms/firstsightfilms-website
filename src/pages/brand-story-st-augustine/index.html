Our Approach to Brand Storytelling

Creative vision meets strategic communication.

Brand story videos are a combination of interviews, footage of your work in action, and conversations with leadership that define your "why." The typical length is 1-3 minutes — long enough to tell a complete story, short enough to hold attention.

What makes our approach unique is the combination of our individual strengths: Diego's creative eye and attention to cinematography paired with Trista's skill in communication, seeing the big picture, and understanding how the pieces fit together.

The result? A visual story that is on brand, visually stunning, and aligned with where your organization is headed.

How It Works

Our brand story process starts with understanding who you are.

1

Discover

We dig into your mission, values, audience, and what makes you different. This shapes the entire story we'll tell.

2

Script

We develop interview questions and a visual approach that brings your story to life authentically — no scripts to memorize.

3

On production day, we interview leadership and team members, capture b-roll of your work, and document what makes you unique.

Deliver
