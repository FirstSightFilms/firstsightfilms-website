1% for the Planet: Stories We've Helped Tell

Partnering with conservation organizations to amplify their impact.

As part of our 1% for the Planet commitment, we've partnered with conservation organizations to help them tell the stories they need to tell. Our process starts with virtual meetings to understand their needs, then asking where they think is best for us to film — they know their home better than anyone. Then we take care of the rest.

Rocky Mountain Conservancy

We created a documentary-style video for Rocky Mountain Conservancy that they proudly presented at the 2025 Friends Alliance — an annual conference for philanthropic groups supporting national parks across the United States.

Friends of Acadia

Friends of Acadia needed a story video describing their volunteer program — helping others see how this ongoing effort supports infrastructure throughout Acadia National Park, bridging the gap between the park's needs and its staffing resources.

Live Wildly & Florida State Park Foundation

We're particularly proud of our collaboration with Live Wildly and the Florida State Park Foundation — a 3-minute brand story video telling the story of how Meredith Budd, Deputy Director of Live Wildly, and Julia Gill Woodward, CEO of the Florida State Park Foundation, joined forces to unify their organizations toward their shared goal: supporting the preservation of wild Florida and the Florida Wildlife Corridor.
