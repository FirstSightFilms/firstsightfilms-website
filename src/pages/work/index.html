Our Work

First Sight Films is a video and photography company in St. Augustine, Florida. We produce corporate films, event and concert coverage, brand stories, and commercial photography for businesses, venues, and nonprofits across Northeast Florida — from the St. Augustine Amphitheatre to Flagler College. This is our work: every page a real project, with real footage, real clients, and real results.

What we do

Video and photography, often on the same shoot — built for social, web, and broadcast.
