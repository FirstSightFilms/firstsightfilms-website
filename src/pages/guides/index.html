Event Coverage Guides — St. Augustine & Northeast Florida

Practical guides for anyone planning, producing, or booking coverage for an event in Northeast Florida. We're First Sight Films — house photographer for the St. Augustine Amphitheatre, main photographer for the Sing Out Loud and Fort Mose festivals, and the conference photographer for the RSA Conference six years running. We've covered 50+ events with one team shooting both photo and video. Everything we've learned about capturing them well — what it costs, how to plan it, which venues work how, and what to ask before you book — lives here.
