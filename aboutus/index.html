At First Sight Films, we believe every story has the power to connect, to reveal what’s shared between people, the places they love, and the world they shape. Through thoughtful production and creative direction, we help organizations communicate what matters most — their work, their values, and their impact.

  • Our mission is to amplify stories that connect people, place, and planet using the power of storytelling to deepen connection, spark curiosity, and move others to act with intention.

  • Our vision is to redefine what meaningful storytelling looks like by  creating content that connects people, place, and planet while setting a new standard for creativity, quality, and impact.

  • Integrity

    We stay grounded in honesty, trust, and accountability — the roots that sustain lasting relationships and authentic storytelling.

    Adaptability

    Like nature, we evolve with every season. We stay flexible, creative, and resilient, ready to meet change with curiosity and purpose.

    Connection

    We celebrate the stories that tie people to place and planet, knowing that every project is an opportunity to strengthen community and understanding.

    Purposeful Impact

    We create with intention — using our craft to uplift others, give back, and leave each place better than we found it.

    Stewardship

    We draw inspiration from the natural world and honor it through our choices, from the stories we tell to the way we operate as a business.

Our Team

  • Diego Cerquera

    Diego is a creative director, cinematographer, and content strategist with 10 years of experience crafting stories that connect people, place, and planet. He specializes in cinematic storytelling that helps organizations communicate their mission with clarity, emotion, and purpose. Guided by a thoughtful and collaborative approach, Diego works closely with clients to translate their vision into content and films that resonate deeply and create lasting impact. His work blends creative vision with technical precision to produce content that inspires connection and moves audiences to action.

  • Trista Brophy Cerquera, Ph.D.

    Trista leads operations, client strategy, and storytelling, helping shape narratives that drive impact and resonate with audiences. She guides projects from concept through delivery, overseeing production logistics, managing timelines, and ensuring every detail supports the story being told. Her background in environmental science, risk management, and resilience planning shapes a production style that prioritizes smooth operations, safe execution, and low-impact workflows. Drawing on her experience in science policy and communication, Trista brings a sharp analytical lens to her work as a photographer and creative producer, crafting content that is purposeful, strategic, and compelling.

A FULL SERVICE CREATIVE TEAM

First Sight Films operates with an incredibly talented team of videographers, editors, drone operators, sound engineers, graphic designers, and production specialists. This team allows us to take on projects of any scale with efficiency and creativity while maintaining the personal attention that sets us apart.
