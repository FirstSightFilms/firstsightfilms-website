Architectural Photography

Project at a glance

  • Clients: St. Augustine Amphitheatre, Ponte Vedra Concert Hall, Fort Mose Historic State Park, Flagler College, Quatrefoil Associates, Walk-On's
  • Session: typically 4–6 hours on-site
  • Lighting: 0 to 4 lights, matched to natural light + intended feel
  • Technique: exposure bracketing for dark interiors, merged in Adobe Lightroom Classic
  • Use: web, print, social

First Sight Films has shot architectural photography for some of Northeast Florida's most recognized spaces. Some frames focus purely on the architecture; others show people interacting with the space, depending on what the image needs to do.

We start with the question that drives everything: where will this live, and what's it for? An ad, an educational or instructional piece, a design concept, a mailer, an email campaign. Sometimes a client doesn't know every use yet, so we shoot to cover them. From there we schedule the session.

How we light it depends on the space and the feel we're after, balanced against the available natural light. For darker interiors like the Ponte Vedra Concert Hall, we use exposure bracketing — capturing the same composition at several exposure levels, then merging them in Adobe Lightroom Classic into one clean, evenly lit frame.

Our Approach to Photography

First Sight Films plans every commercial shoot around one question: where will these images be used? We build the shoot to serve that — an ad, an e-commerce listing, a catalog, a social campaign — so the photos work in the channels you actually need, not just in a gallery.

What sets us apart from a typical photographer:

  • We run full productions, not just a camera. Art direction, production design, and on-set crew — we ran a 7-model, two-day apparel campaign for the St. Augustine Amphitheatre.
  • We start with the end use. We plan around where the image lives — web, print, or social — before the shoot, so you don't pay for photos you can't use.
  • We deliver in 72 hours. Most photographers take one to two weeks; we turn edited galleries around in three days.
  • We capture photo and video in one session. Alongside the stills, we shoot behind-the-scenes footage and short social clips — so you leave with content for every channel, not just a photo gallery.
  • We give you full usage rights. A perpetual license to use the images anywhere, including paid ads, while we handle ownership and archiving.
  • Institutions trust us. We're the photographer for the St. Augustine Amphitheatre, Ponte Vedra Concert Hall, and Sing Out Loud Festival.

And we keep the shoot easy — you stay stress-free while we get the shot. On product and brand work we "fix it in pre-production": plan thoroughly, so we have room to chase what's working on the day.

Commercial Photography FAQs

How much does commercial photography cost in St. Augustine?

Event coverage starts at $1,200 for half-day (4 hours) with 100+ edited images. Commercial photography starts at $1,500 for a half-day shoot with 40+ images. Product photoshoots for lifestyle brands and apparel start at $4,500 for a full day. All sessions include edited, high-resolution images delivered digitally.

What types of photography does First Sight Films offer?

We focus on commercial photography: apparel & lifestyle, product (including clean white-background e-commerce stills), brand & team, and architectural/interior. We shoot the merchandise lines for the St. Augustine Amphitheatre, Ponte Vedra Concert Hall, and Sing Out Loud, plus brand and architectural work across Northeast Florida.

What makes First Sight Films different from other photographers?

We run real productions, not just a camera — art direction, production design, multi-look planning, and a pre-shoot site visit when it's needed. You get full usage rights, and we plan around where the images will actually be used so they work in every channel.

Do you provide models for apparel and lifestyle shoots?

You can bring your own models, or we'll source and coordinate them for an additional $50–120 per hour per model, depending on their experience. The Amphitheatre's Fall/Winter line ran 7 models across two days.

Do you handle art direction and production?

Yes. Larger shoots get an art director, production designer, and on-set crew, plus a pre-shoot site visit so looks and locations are locked before the day.

Do you shoot in a studio or on location?

Both. We shoot on location — venues, state parks, downtown St. Augustine — and we shoot clean studio-style product stills (we'll rent a studio space when a shoot calls for it). Often both in one production.

Do you shoot white-background product photos for e-commerce?

Yes — clean white-background stills built to drop straight into online-store and vendor listings (we do this for the Amphitheatre's store), plus styled in-field shots of the same products for social and catalogs.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

Standard turnaround is within 72 hours for edited images delivered via online gallery. Faster delivery can be arranged when a launch date depends on it — just let us know up front.

Who owns the photos and how can we use them?

You get full usage rights — a perpetual license across web, print, social, e-commerce, and vendor sites. First Sight Films retains ownership of the photographs. We deliver edited high-res images, not raw files.

Are you insured, and can you provide a certificate of insurance?

Yes — we carry $1 million in general liability insurance and can provide a COI for your venue or property ahead of the shoot, including naming them as additional insured if required.

Do you offer video and photography together?

Yes, we frequently combine photo and video coverage for events and brand projects. Bundling services is more efficient and cost-effective than hiring separate vendors.

What areas do you serve for photography?

We're based in St. Augustine and serve clients throughout Northeast Florida, including Jacksonville, Ponte Vedra, St. Johns County, and Palm Coast. We also travel for larger projects — we've shot in national parks across the country.
