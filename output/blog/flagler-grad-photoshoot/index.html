Flagler College Grad Photo Sessions

Skip the awkward poses and stiff smiles. Our Flagler College grad photo sessions are relaxed, fun, and designed to capture the real you at one of Florida's most beautiful campuses.

By Diego Cerquera

You've spent four years at one of the most stunning campuses in America. Your grad photos should capture that experience without the stress of stiff poses and forced smiles.

As a Flagler College alum (Class of 2007), I know this campus inside and out. I know the best light, the hidden spots, and how to make your session feel like hanging out with a friend rather than a formal photoshoot.

Why Flagler Grad Photos Are Different

Most portrait sessions feel awkward. You're told where to stand, how to tilt your head, and when to smile. By the end, you're exhausted and the photos look like everyone else's.

That's not how we do it.

Our sessions are conversations. We walk the campus together, talk about your time at Flagler, and capture real moments along the way. The result? Photos that actually look like you, not some stiff version of yourself.

Iconic Campus Locations We'll Cover

Flagler College isn't just a school. It's a former luxury hotel built by Henry Flagler in 1888, with Spanish Renaissance architecture that makes every photo look incredible.

During your session, we'll hit the spots that mean something:

  • Ponce Hall Courtyard - The iconic fountain, towers, and Spanish architecture
  • The Rotunda - Tiffany stained glass and hand-painted murals (when accessible)
  • The Ponce de Leon Statue - Classic Flagler photo op at the entrance
  • Campus Gardens - Lush palms and quiet pathways for a more intimate feel
  • Your favorite spot - That bench where you studied, the building where you had your favorite class

What to Expect: Your 1-Hour Session

Here's how it works:

Before the session: We'll chat about your vision, what locations matter to you, and what you want to wear. I handle the timing and logistics so you just show up ready to have fun.

During the session: We spend about an hour walking the campus during golden hour (that perfect late afternoon light). I'll guide you naturally through poses without making anything feel forced. We talk, we laugh, and I capture the real moments in between.

After the session: Your edited gallery is ready within 1 week. Need them faster? We offer 24-hour expedited editing for an additional $100.

What to Wear and Bring

A few tips to get the most out of your session:

  • Bring your cap and gown - Obviously. We'll get those classic graduation shots.
  • Bring a second outfit - A casual look that shows your personality gives us variety.
  • Avoid busy patterns - Solid colors photograph better against Flagler's ornate architecture.
  • Bring props that mean something - Your instrument, sports gear, art supplies, whatever makes you you.

Pricing

We keep it simple:

Flagler Grad Photo Session: $300

  • 1-hour golden hour session on campus
  • Multiple iconic locations
  • 15 professionally edited images
  • Online gallery for easy downloading and sharing
  • 1-week turnaround (24-hour expedite available for +$100)

Book Your Session

Graduation season fills up fast, especially for golden hour slots. If you're graduating this spring or fall, now's the time to lock in your date.

Reach out and let's make your Flagler memories last forever, without the awkward poses.

"Walking through Flagler's campus never gets old. Every session reminds me why I fell in love with this place 20 years ago."

Diego Cerquera, Flagler College Class of 2007
