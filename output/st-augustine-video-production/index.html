How We Work

The same process on every project, whatever the service.

  1. Discovery call — we start with a focused call to map your goals, audience, and timeline, so the shoot day runs efficiently and revisions stay minimal.
  2. Pre-production — we handle creative direction and a shoot plan, including locations, talent, and a shot list, before anyone picks up a camera.
  3. Production — a full crew films on the day, not just a camera operator, capturing the main moments and the details in between.
  4. Post-production — editing, color grading, music licensing, and revisions, all handled in-house by the same team.
  5. Delivery — you get a final master plus social cutdowns, delivered ready to publish, built to perform from social feeds to broadcast.

St. Augustine Video Production FAQs

What video production services do you offer in St. Augustine?

Corporate & conference coverage, brand-story films, event & festival coverage, and commercial photography — often video and photo from the same shoot.

What areas do you serve?

St. Augustine and St. Johns County, plus Jacksonville, Ponte Vedra, and Northeast Florida.

How much does video production cost?

Event photo starts at $1,200 and a video day at $2,500. Our Event Story package — a 2-person photo and video crew — is $5,500, with a 7-business-day Rush at $7,500, and multi-camera multi-day Full Coverage from $9,500. Every project includes up to three rounds of revisions.

How fast do we get the finished video?

A short social recap (20–30 seconds) comes within 72 hours. Full films are delivered by package — 2–3 weeks for an Event Story, 3–4 weeks for Full Coverage, or 7 business days on the Rush tier.

Do you shoot both photo and video?

Yes — we regularly cover an event with both, so you get motion and stills from one booking.

What St. Augustine work have you done?

Projects with the St. Augustine Amphitheatre, Sing Out Loud Festival, Flagler College, Fort Mose, and local corporate clients.

How do we book?

A short consult to scope the shoot, then a fixed proposal, then shoot day, then edited delivery.

Do you travel outside St. Augustine?

Yes; we cover Northeast Florida and travel for corporate work.
