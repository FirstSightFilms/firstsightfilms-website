About the Venue

Ponte Vedra Concert Hall is an intimate live music venue located in Ponte Vedra Beach, Florida. With a capacity of approximately 400, the venue hosts national touring acts, comedy shows, and special events throughout the year.

First Sight Films has been a trusted video production partner for the venue, capturing live performances, creating promotional content, and producing social media clips that showcase the unique atmosphere of the space.
