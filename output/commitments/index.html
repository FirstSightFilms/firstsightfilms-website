Commitments and Accountability

Our purpose, values, and the standards we hold ourselves to.

Purpose Statement

We exist to help organizations and communities communicate their work in ways that strengthen understanding, deepen connection, and support the people, places, and ecosystems they serve.

Mission

Our mission is to amplify stories that connect people, place, and planet using the power of storytelling to deepen connection, spark curiosity, and move others to act with intention.

Vision

Our vision is to redefine what meaningful storytelling looks like by creating content that connects people, place, and planet while setting a new standard for creativity, quality, and impact.

Values

Integrity

We stay grounded in honesty, trust, and accountability, the roots that sustain lasting relationships and authentic storytelling.

Adaptability

Like nature, we evolve with every season. We stay flexible, creative, and resilient, ready to meet change with curiosity and purpose.

Connection

We celebrate the stories that tie people to place and planet, knowing that every project is an opportunity to strengthen community and understanding.

Purposeful Impact

We create with intention, using our craft to uplift others, give back, and leave each place better than we found it.

Stewardship

We draw inspiration from the natural world and honor it through our choices, from the stories we tell to the way we operate as a business.

Our Commitments

What We Stand For

At First Sight Films, our work is guided by more than aesthetics. It is shaped by what we believe, how we operate, and the kind of impact we want to have through every project, partnership, and collaboration.

This is the foundation behind our work. It shapes how we create, who we collaborate with, and the kind of impact we aim to have.

We believe that how we work matters just as much as what we create.

How We Stay Accountable

We are building accountability into our business through both daily decisions and external standards. Two key parts of that are our commitment as a 1% for the Planet Business Member and our ongoing pursuit of B Corp Certification.

1% for the Planet Business Member

What it is

A global network of businesses that commit at least 1% of annual revenue to environmental partners.

What it means for us

Giving is built into how we operate. We contribute through both financial support and in-kind creative work that helps mission-driven organizations tell their stories more effectively.

Why it matters

It connects our business growth to real-world impact and keeps our work grounded in something larger than ourselves.

Our B Corp Journey

What it is

A third-party certification that evaluates how a company performs across governance, workers, community, environment, and customers.

Why we are pursuing it

We want our values to be reflected not just in our work, but in how the business itself operates.

What it means

We are building systems for accountability, transparency, and long-term impact. Not as a one-time effort, but as an ongoing standard.

Justice, Equity, Diversity & Inclusion Commitment

At First Sight Films, our work is grounded in equal opportunity, fairness, and respect for human dignity.

We engage clients, collaborators, and partners based on shared values, professionalism, and merit. We do not provide preferential treatment to any individual or group, and we uphold all applicable anti-discrimination laws in how we operate and make decisions.

We believe inclusion means encouraging respectful dialogue and fair participation across every interaction. In our creative work, we approach storytelling with care, aiming to represent people and communities accurately and responsibly.

This commitment is part of how we do business. We review our practices regularly to ensure they remain aligned with our values and the communities we serve—supporting work that strengthens connection between people, place, and the broader world we share.

The Declaration of Interdependence

The company commits to the shared collective purpose of the B Corp community, as set out in the Declaration of Interdependence:

We envision a global economy that uses business as a force for good. This economy is comprised of a new type of corporation — the B Corporation — which is purpose-driven and creates benefit for all stakeholders, not just shareholders.

As Certified B Corporations and leaders of this emerging economy, we believe:

  • That we must be the change we seek in the world.
  • That all business ought to be conducted as if people and place mattered.
  • That, through their products, practices, and profits, businesses should aspire to do no harm and benefit all.

To do so requires that we act with the understanding that we are each dependent upon another and thus responsible for each other and future generations.

Why This Matters

Working with First Sight Films means working with a team that is intentional about both the work and the way it is done.

  • We care about alignment, clarity, and long-term value.
  • We approach storytelling as both a craft and a responsibility.

Report a Concern

We are committed to treating people, communities, and the environments we work in with care and respect. If you have a concern about our conduct, working relationships, environmental practices, or how individuals or communities are represented in our work, we want to hear from you.

You can contact us directly at impact@firstsightfilms.com, speak with the Managing Director, or submit a concern anonymously using this form.

We review all submissions thoughtfully and in good faith. You may report concerns without fear of retaliation, and we will respect your privacy and confidentiality where possible.
