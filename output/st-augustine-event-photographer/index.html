Festival & Concert Photography

Live performances, festival experiences, and brand activations — for the venues and festivals that trust us most.

Project at a glance

  • Client: SJC Cultural Events (SJCCE) — the St. Johns County nonprofit behind the St. Augustine Amphitheatre, Ponte Vedra Concert Hall, Sing Out Loud, and Fort Mose Jazz & Blues
  • Relationship: 4+ years · house photographer for the Amphitheatre · main photographer for Sing Out Loud and Fort Mose
  • Venues & events: St. Augustine Amphitheatre · Ponte Vedra Concert Hall · Sing Out Loud Festival · Fort Mose Jazz & Blues Festival
  • Captured: live performances, festival experience, attendees with branded scenic, sponsor activations (e.g. Live Wildly at Sing Out Loud 2024–2025), behind-the-scenes
  • Delivered: ~450 edited images across a two-day festival · same-day edits pulled while the event is live
  • Rights: perpetual license; First Sight Films retains ownership
  • Use: festival social, next-year ad campaigns, press

First Sight Films is the house photographer for the St. Augustine Amphitheatre and the main photographer for the Sing Out Loud Festival and the Fort Mose Jazz & Blues Festival — all produced by SJC Cultural Events, the nonprofit that also runs the Ponte Vedra Concert Hall. We've worked with SJCCE for four-plus years.

Across their venues and festivals we photograph the whole event — live performances on the main stage, the festival experience, attendees interacting with the branded scenic, and sponsor activations like Live Wildly's at Sing Out Loud in 2024 and 2025, plus behind-the-scenes. For a two-day festival we deliver roughly 450 edited images, and we edit same-day — pulling shots while the event is still live so the festival can post that night.

When the Amphitheatre brought us on to cover the Avett Brothers, we shot both photo and video of the show. Our festival work has run with local news coverage in the Ponte Vedra Recorder and the Jacksonville Business Journal, and SJCCE stores the images to reuse for the next year's paid campaigns and organic social.

Corporate Event Photography

Conference coverage shot for how the images actually get used.

Project at a glance

  • Client: Ten Technical LLC (Alexandria, VA) — FSF subcontracted; event produced by Nth Degree (Atlanta)
  • Event: RSAC 2026 (the RSA Conference) · San Francisco · multi-day · ~40,000 attendees
  • Relationship: 6 years covering this event for Ten Technical
  • Role: on-site event photographer + production support
  • Captured: attendees, breakout sessions, keynote stages — shot to feed next year's digital-signage designs
  • Delivered: ~100 edited images at the end of each day
  • Logistics: travel from St. Augustine with full camera kit · $1M liability + venue COI

First Sight Films photographs the RSA Conference (RSAC) in San Francisco — one of the largest cybersecurity events in the world, drawing roughly 40,000 attendees across multiple venues. We've covered it for six years running.

We're brought in by Ten Technical, the team behind RSAC's digital signage — the screens throughout the venues that show agendas, speaker bios, and next-up-on-stage information (Ten Technical works under the event's producer, Nth Degree). Our job is to photograph the conference as it happens — attendees, breakout sessions, and the keynote stages — and the images do double duty: they document this year's event and become the source material the designers build next year's signage from.

Each day we deliver around 100 edited photographs by end of day, so the team has a usable gallery before the next morning. The assignment runs out of San Francisco, so we travel with our full camera kit and carry $1M in liability with a certificate of insurance for the venue.

Brand & Sponsor Activation Photography

Proof your activation landed — photo and video built for sponsorship ROI.

What activation coverage captures

  • Branded scenic and signage in use — your logo where the crowd actually is
  • Attendees engaging — lines, demos, giveaways, reactions
  • Foot traffic and crowd scale at peak moments
  • Brand ambassadors and staff in action
  • Detail shots — swag, menus, installations — for recap decks and next year's pitch
  • Photo and video from one team, same activation

A brand activation only lives for the hours it's on the floor — unless someone documents it. First Sight Films photographs and films brand and sponsor activations at conferences and festivals so the investment keeps paying off long after the event: in sponsorship recap decks, next year's pitch to leadership, social proof, and the producer's own marketing.

At RSAC 2026 — the 40,000-attendee RSA Conference in San Francisco — we captured activations out on the show floor, from a sponsor's “No AI Zone” coffee bar to branded giveaways and the crowds they drew. The images show what a report can't: the brand in use, the people, and the scale of the moment.

The same coverage carries festival sponsors. At Sing Out Loud we photograph Live Wildly's on-site activation as part of our festival work, giving the sponsor imagery that proves reach and engagement. Whether it's a conference booth or a festival footprint, we shoot the activation the way a sponsor needs to report on it.

Event Photography FAQs

What types of events do you photograph?

Concerts and festivals, corporate conferences, brand & sponsor activations, ribbon cuttings and grand openings, and graduation/commencement. We're the house photographer for the St. Augustine Amphitheatre and the main photographer for Sing Out Loud and Fort Mose.

How much does event photography cost?

Photo coverage starts at $1,200 for a half-day with 100+ edited images; combined photo + video event coverage starts at $5,500. A quick call gets you an exact number.

How soon do I get my photos?

Within 72 hours by default, and same-day when the moment needs it — we turned Walk-On's grand opening around same day and edit festivals live so they post that night.

Do you shoot photo and video together?

Yes — much of our event work is both, one team on stills and video so you're not juggling vendors.

Can you photograph brand and sponsor activations?

Yes. Activation coverage built for sponsorship ROI — the brand in use, attendee engagement, crowd scale — for recap decks, social, and next-year pitches.

Do you travel outside St. Augustine?

Yes. We cover events across Northeast Florida and travel nationally — we've photographed the RSA Conference in San Francisco for six years.

Yes — $1M general liability, with a certificate of insurance for your venue, including additional insured if required.
