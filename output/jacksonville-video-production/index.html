Jacksonville Video Production FAQs

How much does video production cost in Jacksonville?

Video production in Jacksonville typically ranges from $2,500 to $10,000 depending on scope. Corporate videos, event coverage, and brand stories each have different requirements. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your goals.

Do you travel to Jacksonville for video projects?

Yes, we're based in St. Augustine — just 40 minutes south of Jacksonville. We regularly work with Jacksonville businesses, nonprofits, and venues. No travel fees for projects within the Jacksonville metro area.

What types of video production do you offer in Jacksonville?

We offer corporate video, event coverage, brand story videos, social media content, and commercial photography. Our clients in Jacksonville include businesses, nonprofits, government agencies, and cultural organizations.

How long does a video project take from start to finish?

Most projects take 3-6 weeks from kickoff to final delivery. This includes strategy, pre-production, filming, and editing. Event coverage and quick-turnaround projects can be delivered faster when needed.

Can you handle large corporate events in Jacksonville?

Yes, we have experience with multi-day conferences, corporate galas, and large-scale events. We can provide multi-camera coverage, live streaming, and same-day social media content delivery.
