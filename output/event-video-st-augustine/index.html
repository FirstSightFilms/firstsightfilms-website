Our Approach to Event Coverage

Purposeful filming, not just pointing and recording.

We capture the most important part of any event: the people, the experience, the vibe. This is different than just pointing a camera at anything and hitting record.

How It Works

Our event video process is designed to stay out of your way while capturing everything that matters.

1

Align

We start by understanding your goals. What story do you want to tell? Who needs to see this? That clarity shapes everything we capture.

Event Video FAQs

How much does event video production cost in St. Augustine?

Event video coverage starts at $1,200 for half-day coverage (up to 4 hours) and $2,400 for full-day coverage (up to 8 hours). Multi-day events like festivals and conferences are quoted based on scope. All packages include a highlight reel, raw footage, and social media clips. Additional costs may apply for extensive editing, expedited delivery, or complete speaker capture.

How far in advance should I book event video coverage?

We recommend booking 4-6 weeks in advance for most events. For large festivals or multi-day conferences, 2-3 months notice helps ensure availability and allows time for proper planning and venue scouting.

What makes First Sight Films different from other event videographers?

We focus on capturing the people, the experience, and the vibe — not just pointing a camera and hitting record. Every shot is driven by a deeper understanding of your goals: to tell the story of people at your event. We've been the go-to video partner for the St. Augustine Amphitheatre for 2+ years and the photographers for Sing Out Loud Festival.

What's included in an event highlight reel?

A typical highlight reel is 2-5 minutes and includes the best moments from your event: key speakers, audience reactions, venue atmosphere, and any special performances or announcements. We also provide short-form clips optimized for Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

Do you offer live streaming for events?

Yes, we offer live streaming services for conferences, corporate events, and special occasions. This includes multi-camera switching, graphics, and streaming to platforms like YouTube, Facebook, or private platforms.

How quickly will I receive my event video?

Standard turnaround is 2 weeks for a polished highlight reel. For events that need faster delivery — like same-day social media content — we offer expedited options. Let us know your timeline when booking.
