Festival Coverage: Sing Out Loud and Fort Mose Jazz and Blues

Annual · Francis Field, downtown St. Augustine, and Fort Mose Historic State Park

Project at a glance

  • Client: SJC Cultural Events, producer of both festivals
  • Sing Out Loud: month-long free showcase downtown plus the ticketed Live Wildly main event at Francis Field
  • Fort Mose Jazz and Blues: night concerts at Fort Mose Historic State Park, site of the first free Black settlement in what is now the United States
  • Delivered: per-day recap films, short social clips, and same-day photo edits so the festivals can post while the show is still running
  • Festival scale, for context: Sing Out Loud drew 34,000 plus attendees across the month, and the festival's partnership with Live Wildly has raised $130,000 plus for Florida wildlife corridor conservation

St. Johns County's two signature festivals both happen within a few miles of our front door, and First Sight Films covers both for SJCCE. The festival numbers above belong to the festivals, and we cite them because they show the scale our crew works at: national headliners and tens of thousands of attendees, with deliverables due before the next act takes the stage.

The full case studies, including crew breakdowns and press pickup, live on our event video coverage page. If you run a festival or a large public event anywhere in the county, start there.

St. Johns County Video Production FAQs

How much does video production cost in St. Johns County?

Most event projects run from $1,200 to $9,500 plus. Event photography starts at $1,200 for a half day, a video day starts at $2,500, and combined photo and video coverage starts at $5,500, with a Rush option at $7,500 and multi-day Full Coverage from $9,500. Corporate and brand films are quoted to scope after a 15-minute call.

What areas of St. Johns County do you serve?

All of it. St. Augustine, St. Augustine Beach, Ponte Vedra Beach, Nocatee, World Golf Village, Fruit Cove, Julington Creek, Palm Valley, and St. Johns. We are based in St. Augustine and charge no travel fees anywhere in the county.

What types of video do you produce?

Corporate and conference video, event coverage, brand story films, social media content, and venue promotional work, plus event and commercial photography. Our county clients include SJC Cultural Events, the St. Johns County Chamber of Commerce, the St. Johns Cultural Council, and St. Johns County Government.

Can you cover events at the St. Augustine Amphitheatre or Ponte Vedra Concert Hall?

Yes. SJC Cultural Events, the nonprofit that operates both venues, has kept First Sight Films on call for four years for video, photography, editing, and whatever the season needs. We know both rooms and their crews, down to the audio boards.

Do you charge travel fees within St. Johns County?

No. The county is our home base, so travel anywhere from Ponte Vedra Beach to St. Augustine Beach is included.

How fast is your turnaround?

For events, edited photos and a 20 to 30 second social recap arrive within 72 hours. Simple projects deliver in 3 to 5 days. Brand films and multi-day productions take 3 to 6 weeks. Our Event Story Rush tier guarantees full delivery in 7 business days.

Who owns the footage, and can we use it commercially?

First Sight Films retains ownership of the raw footage, and you receive a perpetual license for commercial use: your website, your social channels, your paid advertising, and anything else your marketing needs. You never pay again to use what we made for you.

Are you insured? Can you provide a certificate of insurance?

Yes. We carry $1 million in general liability coverage and can supply a certificate of insurance, including naming your venue as additional insured. County venues and resorts routinely require this, and we handle it as part of booking.

How do you handle audio at live events?

With redundancy. We record multiple audio inputs and take a direct feed from the venue's audio board when one is available. Lav mics go at the podium when speakers need them. Bad audio ruins good footage, so we never rely on a single source.

What happens if equipment fails during our event?

Nothing you would notice. We carry backups for cameras, audio, power, and media, and we shoot multi-camera, so no single failure can cost you a moment that matters.

How far in advance should we book?

As early as you can once your date is firm. Fall festival season and spring event dates in the county fill first. Reach out with your date and we will tell you honestly whether we can hold it.

Why hire a local St. Johns County company instead of a Jacksonville crew?

Familiarity and speed. We have worked the county's venues for years and can scout a new location in an afternoon, and we charge no travel fees. When the Consulate General of Ireland needed a videographer here, two out-of-area vendors went silent for three weeks. We answered within 30 minutes.

Do you offer photography as well as video?

Yes, both event and commercial photography. Event photography starts at $1,200 for a half day with 100 plus edited images. Commercial photography for product, apparel, architectural, and brand imagery starts at $1,500 and is a separate service with its own page.

What makes First Sight Films different from hiring a freelancer?

Scope and accountability. A single shooter can film your event, but everything else still lands on you: editing, color, music licensing, backup gear, insurance paperwork. We carry all of it in house, and 46 five-star Google reviews say it shows.
