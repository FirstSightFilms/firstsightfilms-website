St. Johns County Video Production FAQs

How much does video production cost in St. Johns County?

Video production in St. Johns County typically runs from $1,200 to $9,500+ depending on scope. Event photo starts at $1,200, a video day at $2,500, and full photo-and-video event coverage at $5,500. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your goals.

What areas of St. Johns County do you serve?

We serve all of St. Johns County including St. Augustine, St. Augustine Beach, Ponte Vedra Beach, Nocatee, World Golf Village, Fruit Cove, Julington Creek, and surrounding communities. We're based in St. Augustine with no travel fees anywhere in the county.

What types of video production do you offer in St. Johns County?

We offer corporate video, event coverage, brand story videos, social media content, and commercial photography. Our clients include the St. Johns County Chamber of Commerce, St. Johns Cultural Council, St. Johns County Government, and businesses throughout the county.

Do you work with St. Johns County organizations?

Yes, we have ongoing partnerships with key St. Johns County organizations including the St. Johns County Chamber of Commerce, St. Johns Cultural Council, SJCCE Inc. (which operates the St. Augustine Amphitheatre and Ponte Vedra Concert Hall), and St. Johns County Government.

Can you cover events at St. Johns County venues?

Absolutely. We're the go-to video and photography partner for St. Johns County's premier venues including the St. Augustine Amphitheatre, Ponte Vedra Concert Hall, and various event spaces throughout the county. We've covered hundreds of events across St. Johns County.
