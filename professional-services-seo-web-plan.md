## Executive Summary

This report analyzes professional services industries (law, accounting, engineering, architecture) in Duval County (Jacksonville) and St. Johns County (St. Augustine, Ponte Vedra) for video production market opportunity. All data sourced from government (.gov), industry associations (.org), and credible business publications.

**Key Finding:** The combined market represents 5,000+ professional services practitioners with specific sub-specialty concentrations driven by demographics: elder law and estate planning thrive due to St. Johns County's 21% 65+ population; construction/real estate law dominates due to $6.5B+ development pipeline; personal injury leads by volume in Duval County (37,230 crashes in 2024).
